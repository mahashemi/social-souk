# SocialSouk — Vision & Mission

## Vision
To be the world's leading Islamic social commerce platform — a trusted digital space where Muslims across the globe trade, connect, and build community in harmony with their faith.

## Mission
SocialSouk combines the connectivity of social media with the functionality of a marketplace, empowering Muslims to buy, sell, and chat in an environment built on halal values, transparency, and brotherhood (ukhuwwah).

## Tagline
**"Trade with Barakah."** — Where community meets commerce, and every transaction carries Allah's blessing.

---

## Core Values
1. **Halal First** — Every listing is community-reviewed for halal compliance.
2. **Transparency** — Honest descriptions, real reviews, no deception (gharar).
3. **Ukhuwwah (Brotherhood)** — A community, not just a marketplace.
4. **Barakah** — Encouraging fair pricing and honest trade as commanded in Islam.
5. **Privacy & Dignity** — User data is protected; modesty is respected in all content.

---

## Target Audience
- **Primary:** Muslim buyers and sellers aged 18–45 globally
- **Secondary:** Halal-conscious non-Muslims interested in ethical trade
- **Niche:** Islamic product artisans, home-based sellers, halal small businesses

## Key Features (MVP)
- User registration & profile with bio, city, phone
- Create listings (title, description, price, category, images)
- Social feed (see recent listings from followed users)
- Follow / unfollow users
- Direct messaging (chat) between buyer and seller
- Search & filter by category, city, price
- Halal badge system (self-declared + community-verified)
- Reviews & ratings on completed trades
